## Late Parity Audit

The read-only audit completed after the initial handoff commit. Treat these
behaviors as part of the current migration contract:

- Product: categorized home-textile/garment choices, unfinished fabric products,
  editable dimensions for flexible products, and fixed-size readouts.
- Material: up to five ordered selections in mix mode, selection-limit feedback,
  and grid or organic patchwork modes.
- Technique: deadstock/reclaimed surface-finish rules, automatic removal and
  locking of digital print for reclaimed material, and laser etching.
- Pattern: preset/text/upload input modes, reference-image preview, explicit
  loading/errors, generated artwork, and surface-mode labels.
- Layout: repeat mode, placement, scale, rotation, and per-motif scale/rotation/
  offset controls; all changes invalidate the generated render.
- Render: explicit generation/re-generation with loading/errors and the current
  artwork image supplied as model input.
- Tech pack: jsPDF export, embedded artwork/render images, surface rule,
  construction notes, producer details, and browser-print fallback.
- Preview/output: mix-aware swatches, organic clip paths, dynamic dimensions,
  circular labels, producer ranking, and impact metrics.
- Generation API: preserve `POST /api/generate` with
  `{ prompt, image?, mode? }`, success `{ image, model, mock? }`, typed error
  responses, and a deterministic no-credential fallback until Convex replaces it.
